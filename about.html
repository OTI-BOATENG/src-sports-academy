Building Champions with Passion, Purpose and Pride

Pentvars SRC Sports Academy is a high-performance training environment where young athletes learn sport, leadership and lifelong confidence through elite coaching, community support and a values-driven vision.

About the Academy

Pentvars SRC Sports Academy combines school-based support with professional sports development to create a complete athlete experience. Our programs balance skill development, academic encouragement and personal growth so every student can thrive on and off the field.

We focus on practical training, mental strength, and teamwork while delivering structured programs for soccer, basketball, tennis and multi-sport conditioning.
