Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking and training

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance. For popular time slots and coaches, we suggest booking 1-2 weeks ahead to secure your preferred session.

What should I bring to my session?

Comfortable athletic clothing, water bottle, and towel. We provide all necessary sports equipment. If you have specific gear preferences, please mention it during booking.

Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before your session at no charge. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a 50% fee. Contact us directly for changes.

Do you offer beginner-friendly sessions?

Absolutely! We cater to all skill levels from complete beginners to professionals. Our coaches will assess your level and create an appropriate training plan.

Are there any age restrictions?

We welcome participants from 6 years old and up. For children under 12, we recommend parental supervision. Some sports may have specific age requirements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, card payments, EFT transfers, and mobile payments. Payment is required at the time of booking confirmation.
